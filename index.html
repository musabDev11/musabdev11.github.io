About

I'm a 12-year-old horror author and entrepreneur from Pakistan, proving that age is just a number when it comes to storytelling.

My journey into horror began with a concept for a game that evolved into something far more ambitious—a novella that explores the darkest corners of human nature and cosmic terror.

I believe in building worlds that challenge readers, stories that linger long after the final page, and creating experiences that blur the line between fiction and reality.

Forthcoming Work

THE LAST ORDER

A horror novella that follows Detective Alistair as he investigates a missing girl and uncovers a town built to feed an ancient, terrifying entity. This isn't just another haunted building story—it's institutional cosmic horror where human conspiracy meets forces beyond comprehension.

The Last Order explores themes of complicity, systematic evil, and what happens when civilizations make deals with powers they can never truly control.
